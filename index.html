Energy Zone Heart Rate Calculation

Heart Rate Training Zones

  • AN.. 95-100% (Anaerobic without oxygen). Short bursts of maximum effort. Unsustainable. Burning Carbohydrate.
  • TR.. 85-95% Oxygen Transportation. Working hard. Unsustainable for long periods.
  • AT.. 80-85% (Anaerobic Threshold. Harder work. On the aerobic limit. Pushing into the anaerobic area.
  • UT1.. 70-80% Utilisation 1. Heavy aerobic work using more oxygen.
  • UT2.. 55-70% Utilisation 2. Light aerobic, low intensity work. Sustainable and uses mainly fat for fuel.
