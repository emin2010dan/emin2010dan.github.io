Stroke Rate Information
What is best stroke rate?

There are training rates and there are racing rates. For most of your training it's good to use lower rates like 18 to 20 as they help build power, technique and control. When you want to go as fast as you can (which I would suggest should be no more than once or twice a week depending on how many sessions you do...) then you can go as high as feels right for getting the best pace.(As long as it's not too high as that just wastes energy go up and down the rail too often). Rods ‘Returners’ 10k Training plan
Do you want to row 10k?

Suitable for new starters, first time 10k’s and rowers getting back into it. This is not an advanced level plan so heart rate caps are kept below 92.5% of Maximum for interval sessions and 70% for the steady rows. Someone looking for a faster 10k than previous efforts would not have a cap on some sessions but it would not be sustainable in the long term. This plan assumes a return to progressive training after recovering from the 10k Time Trial. thediamonds.freeforums.net
